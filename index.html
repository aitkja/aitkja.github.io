Our Simple Process

Step 1

Browse & Inquire

Explore our portfolio for inspiration or bring your unique idea. Use the quote form to tell us about your project.

Step 2

Collaborate & Design

We'll discuss the details with you. For custom work, you'll receive a digital CAD design proof for approval.

Step 3

Confirm Quote

Once the design is approved, we'll provide a final quote based on materials, complexity, and quantity.

Step 4

Production

Our precision lasers get to work, crafting your items with care based on the approved design.

Step 5

Completion & Delivery

We'll notify you when your order is ready for shipping or local collection from the Riverbend area.

About Forest City Laser

We specialize in custom wood engravings, offering fast turnaround times for wood, leather, and acrylic engraving and cutting. Our primary market includes weddings, events, corporate items, personalized gifts, and signage. We also create industrial lamicoids and signs.

Forest City Laser is proudly Canadian and based in London, Ontario.
