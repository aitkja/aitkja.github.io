Our Simple Process

Step 1

Browse & Inquire

Explore our portfolio for inspiration or bring your unique idea. Use the quote form to tell us about your project.

Step 2

Collaborate & Design

We'll discuss the details with you. For custom work, you'll receive a digital CAD design proof for approval.

Step 3

Invoice & Payment

Once the design is approved, we'll send you an invoice with final pricing. Payment is required before production begins to secure your order.

Step 4

Production

Our precision lasers get to work, crafting your items with care based on the approved design.

Step 5

Completion & Delivery

We'll notify you when your order is ready for shipping or local collection from the Riverbend area.

About Forest City Laser

Forest City Laser was born from the creative partnership between an engineer and an interior designer who wanted to combine technical precision with beautiful design. Together, we discovered our passion for bringing people's visions to life using cutting-edge laser technology.

What started as our shared love for creating custom pieces has grown into a business dedicated to transforming ideas into tangible, personalized works of art. We blend engineering expertise with design sensibility to deliver exceptional results for every project.

We specialize in custom wood engravings, offering fast turnaround times for wood, leather, and acrylic engraving and cutting. Our primary market includes weddings, events, corporate items, personalized gifts, and signage. We also create industrial lamicoids and signs for businesses throughout the region.

Proudly Canadian and based in London, Ontario, we've been serving the greater Forest City community since 2021 with the perfect marriage of technical innovation and creative design.
